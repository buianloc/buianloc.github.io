Recipe on eggs, bacon and vegetables

Recipe Title: Eggs, bacon and vegetables



Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 6 slices of bacon, chopped
- 1 bell pepper, diced
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional: shredded cheese for topping

Instructions:
1. In a skillet, cook the chopped bacon over medium heat until crispy. Remove excess grease if necessary.
2. Add diced onion and bell pepper to the skillet. Saut√© until vegetables are tender.
3. Push the vegetables and bacon to the side, creating space in the skillet. Crack the eggs into the open space.
4. Cook the eggs to your liking, whether it's sunny-side-up, over-easy, or scrambled with the vegetables and bacon.
5. Add cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook briefly until they soften slightly.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you like, sprinkle shredded cheese over the top.
7. Serve the egg, bacon, and vegetable mixture hot, either on its own or with your favorite toast.

Enjoy your delicious and hearty breakfast!
