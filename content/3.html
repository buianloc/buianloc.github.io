Beef and Vegetable Stir-Fry with a Fried Egg

Recipe Title: Beef and Vegetable Stir-Fry with a Fried Egg

Ingredients:
- 1 pound beef (such as sirloin or flank steak), thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 small onion, sliced
- 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 cup snap peas, ends trimmed
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch, dissolved in 2 tablespoons water
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- 4 eggs
- Sesame seeds, for garnish (optional)
- Fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
3. Add the beef slices to the skillet and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove the beef from the skillet and set it aside.
4. In the same skillet, add the sliced onion, bell pepper, carrot, broccoli florets, and snap peas
